Mountains

Mountains are natural wonders that have captivated human imagination
for centuries. They are landforms that rise significantly above the
surrounding terrain, often forming peaks, ridges and valleys.

Types of Mountains:

  1. Fold Mountains.
  2. Volcanic Mountains.
  3. Block Mountains.
  4. Dome Mountains.
  5. Plateau Mountains.

Characteristics:

  1. Elevation:
    Varies from 300 to 8848 meters
  2. Slope:
    Steep to gentle
  3. Climate:
    Varies with elevation and latitude

Famous Mountains:

  1. Mount Everest
  2. K2
  3. Mount Kilimanjaro
  4. Mount Elbrus
  5. Denali
